Some meals belong exactly where they are served. Beneath an almond tree beside the sea. Overlooking a quiet valley. On the veranda of an old estate. The menu changes with the season, the people preparing it and the ingredients the islands offer that day. Salt & Sorrel is less about dining than about being welcomed — an unhurried gathering where conversation, place and food belong to one another.

The Vision

A table that tastes like the island

Salt & Sorrel is an elevated Vincentian tasting experience celebrating local ingredients, fruit wine, savoury pairings, sweets and the sensory character of Saint Vincent & the Grenadines — sorrel, breadfruit, cocoa, tropical fruit and herbs, presented with restraint and care around a single guided table.

Development Status

Salt & Sorrel is currently a concept in development, shaped in conversation with local producers, cooks and growers. Menus, pairings and pricing throughout this page are illustrative and subject to change.

Visitors can already see Saint Vincent. Salt & Sorrel gives them a way to taste, understand and remember it.

Three Ways to Experience

Salt & Sorrel, in three formats

  • 01The Villa Table — a tasting hosted privately at a guest's villa or residence.
  • 02The Destination Table — a recurring or seasonal tasting hosted at a partner property.
  • 03Fly & Taste — Salt & Sorrel paired with a SALT Air flight as a single day's arc.

The Table

Gather. Taste. Linger.

Gather

A table, not a menu card

Guests arrive to a table already set — glassware, linen and the first pour waiting, the evening framed before the first course begins.

Taste

Fruit wine, breadfruit, cocoa and salt

A sequence of small pours and savoury pairings, moving between sorrel, mango, passion-fruit and tamarind, each paired with a local savoury or sweet moment.

Linger

The evening doesn't end at dessert

Coffee, chocolate and conversation close the table, with no fixed point at which guests are expected to leave.

The Guest Experience

The guest journey

Arrival

Guests enter a beautifully prepared setting, table already dressed.

Welcome Pour

A chilled sorrel infusion or first fruit wine.

The Island Introduction

A short, unhurried note on the geography and produce behind the table.

The Tasting Flight

A sequence of savoury pairings across several small wine pours.

The Artisan Board

Cheese, fruit, cocoa, honey and local accompaniments, shared at the centre of the table.

Sweet Finish

A small frozen dessert flight, followed by coffee and chocolate.

Departure

Guests leave with a fuller sense of what the island grows, makes and eats.

For Hospitality Partners

How Salt & Sorrel could live at your property

Salt & Sorrel is designed to sit comfortably within an existing food and beverage programme rather than compete with it — a signature evening rather than a standing menu change.

What Salt & Sorrel May Provide

  • Menu and pairing development
  • Local producer curation
  • Tasting host and guest storytelling
  • Printed tasting cards and table styling

What a Partner May Provide

  • Venue, kitchen and refrigeration access
  • Tables, seating and shade
  • Guest marketing and concierge sales
  • Booking support and a weather backup location

Closing Vision

A table that could exist nowhere else

Salt & Sorrel is in development. We are seeking thoughtful hospitality, venue and producer partners to help shape The Vincentian Tasting Table.
