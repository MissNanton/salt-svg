The Vision

Every element, chosen for you

SALT Private designs bespoke experiences for villas, yachts, couples, families and private groups — drawing on the full SALT repertoire and shaped entirely around the setting and the guest, rather than a fixed format.

A SALT Private day might combine a scenic flight, a tasting table, a craft encounter and a quiet evening under the stars — or none of these, built instead around whatever the guest actually wants.

The setting is not a backdrop. With SALT Private, it is the starting point for everything else.

Three Ways to Experience

SALT Private, in three formats

  • 01A Single Occasion — one bespoke moment built around a birthday, anniversary or arrival.
  • 02A Villa or Yacht Itinerary — several SALT experiences combined across a stay.
  • 03An Ongoing Arrangement — a returning guest or repeat charter with a standing SALT relationship.

The Process

Listen. Design. Deliver.

Listen

Understanding the guest first

Every SALT Private day begins with a conversation — about the setting, the occasion, and what the guest actually wants from their time in the islands.

Design

Drawing on the full SALT repertoire

Elements from across SALT Air, Salt & Sorrel, SALT Studio and SALT After Dark are combined, adapted or set aside entirely to fit the setting and the guest.

Final combination depends on setting, season and guest preference.

Deliver

Hosted with the same care throughout

Once agreed, the day is delivered with a single point of contact, so the guest experiences one considered arc rather than several disconnected bookings.

The Guest Experience

The guest journey

Initial Conversation

A discussion of the setting, occasion and guest preferences, directly or through a villa manager, yacht crew or advisor.

Proposal

A short, considered outline of what the day or stay could include.

Confirmation

Details, timings and logistics are finalised ahead of arrival.

Delivery

The experience is hosted on-site, with a single SALT point of contact throughout.

Follow-Up

A brief note afterward, and the option to arrange a future SALT day.

For Hospitality Partners

How SALT Private supports your guests

SALT Private is designed to work closely with villa managers, yacht crews and travel advisors who already know their guests well — SALT designs around the brief they provide, not around a fixed package.

What SALT Private May Provide

  • Bespoke itinerary design
  • Coordination across every SALT experience
  • A single point of contact throughout
  • Discreet, considered hosting

What a Partner May Provide

  • Guest brief and preferences
  • Venue, villa or vessel access
  • Local logistics support
  • Ongoing guest relationship context

Closing Vision

A day built around one guest, not many
