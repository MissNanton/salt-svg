The Vision

An archipelago, read as one shape

SALT Air is a scenic aerial exploration of Saint Vincent & the Grenadines, revealing the archipelago through flight, geography, storytelling and wonder. Seen from above, the relationship between sea, reef and land becomes legible in a way no map or itinerary quite captures on the ground.

This is a concept currently in development. Aircraft, exact routing, timings and any aviation partnerships are not yet confirmed — what follows describes the intended shape of the experience, not a finished, bookable product.

Guests already know these islands from the water. SALT Air lets them meet the same islands from the sky.

Three Ways to Experience

SALT Air, in three formats

  • 01The Full Safari — a complete scenic flight across the Grenadines, paired with a SALT experience on arrival.
  • 02Private Charter Add-On — a scenic routing arranged around an existing villa, yacht or hotel stay.
  • 03A Signature Pairing — a shorter flight introducing a longer SALT day, such as Salt & Sorrel or SALT After Dark.

The Flight

Ascend. Unfold. Return.

Ascend

Saint Vincent falls away

Guests board a small aircraft near the coast. Within minutes, the island's own shape — mountain, bay, town, reef — becomes visible whole for the first time.

Aircraft, departure point and exact timings to be confirmed with an aviation partner.

Unfold

The islands, one after another

The heart of the flight: a low, unhurried pass across the Grenadines, tracing reef, channel and coastline as the archipelago reveals its own logic from above.

Route illustrative only, subject to aviation arrangements and weather.

Return

An arrival, not just a landing

The flight closes not with a return to routine, but a handover into whatever comes next — a Salt & Sorrel table, a SALT After Dark evening, or simply the rest of a private day.

The Guest Experience

The guest journey

Arrival & Briefing

A short welcome and safety briefing at the point of departure.

Boarding

Guests board a small aircraft, seated for clear views on both sides where possible.

The Ascent

Saint Vincent's coastline and mountain interior open up within the first minutes of flight.

The Island Traverse

A guided pass across the Grenadines, with light narration on geography, history and the islands below.

Descent & Landing

A gentler return leg, giving a second, later-light view of the same landscape.

Onward

Where arranged, the flight hands directly into another SALT experience or back to the guest's own day.

For Hospitality Partners

How SALT Air could extend a stay

SALT Air is well suited to properties with guests who arrive by air, or who already think of a stay as beginning before check-in. It can act as an arrival moment, a signature excursion, or a pairing with another SALT experience.

What SALT Air May Provide

  • Concept and narration design
  • Coordination with an aviation partner
  • Guest briefing materials
  • Pairing design with other SALT experiences

What a Partner May Provide

  • Guest referral and concierge sales
  • Ground transfer coordination
  • Departure point or helipad access, where applicable
  • Marketing support for a signature offering

Closing Vision

An archipelago worth reading from above

SALT Air is in development. We are seeking thoughtful aviation, hospitality and destination partners to help shape it into a considered, repeatable experience.
