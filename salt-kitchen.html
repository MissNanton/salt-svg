The heart of every home is rarely the dining room. It is the kitchen. Recipes are shared from memory rather than measured from books. Someone chops, someone stirs, someone reaches for another glass while something simmers on the stove. Long before the meal is served, guests have become part of its making. SALT Kitchen invites guests to prepare a Vincentian meal alongside their host before gathering around the table to share it together.

The Vision

A kitchen, opened to guests

SALT Kitchen is a hosted Vincentian cooking experience set in a private villa, where guests prepare a traditional meal alongside their host before sitting down to share it together.

The menu changes with the season and the people cooking it, but the rhythm remains the same: conversation first, cooking together, then a long table shared over dinner. The cooking is only part of the evening. What guests remember is the feeling of being welcomed into a kitchen that, for a few hours, feels like their own.

Guests don't just taste Saint Vincent at SALT Kitchen. They cook it, together, before they eat it.

The Evening

Arrive. Cook. Feast.

Arrive

A villa, a welcome, a glass of wine

Guests are welcomed into a private villa kitchen with a glass of local wine, easy introductions, and a first look at the evening's ingredients and menu.

Cook

A menu made together

Guests prepare a traditional Vincentian meal together under gentle guidance, learning about local ingredients and techniques as they cook.

Feast

A table set for the view

As the final dishes finish cooking, guests return to the terrace for another glass while the table is prepared for dinner. Dinner is plated, considered and shared together, the villa theirs until it's time to go home.

For Hospitality Partners

A memorable evening around the table.

SALT Kitchen complements an existing villa or resort programme by giving guests something many holidays never offer: the chance to prepare, share and enjoy a traditional Vincentian meal together.

Ways to offer SALT Kitchen

  • 01The Villa Kitchen: a hosted cooking evening at a guest's own private villa.
  • 02The Resort Kitchen: a seasonal in-residence version for partner hotels, built around a demonstration kitchen or event space.
  • 03Fly & Feast: SALT Kitchen paired with a SALT Air flight as a single day's arc.

What SALT Kitchen May Provide

  • Menu and technique development
  • Local producer curation
  • Cooking host and guest storytelling
  • Table styling and printed menu cards

What a Partner May Provide

  • Villa kitchen or demonstration space access
  • Cookware, tableware and refrigeration
  • Guest marketing and concierge sales
  • Booking support and a weather backup location

Closing Vision

A kitchen that could exist nowhere else

SALT Kitchen is in development. We are seeking thoughtful villa, resort and producer partners to help shape it.
