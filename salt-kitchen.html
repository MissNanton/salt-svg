The Vision

A kitchen, opened to guests

SALT Kitchen is a hands-on Vincentian cooking experience hosted in a private villa kitchen — part cooking class, part dinner party, part welcome into the islands. Guests arrive to wine and easy conversation before being introduced to the ingredients and the purely Vincentian menu they will make together: callaloo soup, breadfruit, saltfish fritters, roast meats and other island staples, guided course by course.

While the last dishes finish, guests return to the terrace with a drink, the table already being set for the view. Dinner is served plated and considered, eaten together, in a villa made to feel like their own for the evening.

This is a concept currently in development, shaped in conversation with local villa owners, cooks and producers. Menus, pairings and pricing below are illustrative and subject to change.

Guests don't just taste Saint Vincent at SALT Kitchen. They cook it, together, before they eat it.

Three Ways to Experience

SALT Kitchen, in three formats

  • 01The Villa Kitchen — a hosted cooking evening at a guest's own private villa.
  • 02The Resort Kitchen — a seasonal in-residence version for partner hotels, built around a demonstration kitchen or event space.
  • 03Fly & Feast — SALT Kitchen paired with a SALT Air flight as a single day's arc.

For Hospitality Partners

How SALT Kitchen could live at your property

SALT Kitchen is designed to sit comfortably within an existing villa or resort programme rather than compete with it — a signature evening rather than a standing kitchen commitment.

What SALT Kitchen May Provide

  • Menu and technique development
  • Local producer curation
  • Cooking host and guest storytelling
  • Table styling and printed menu cards

What a Partner May Provide

  • Villa kitchen or demonstration space access
  • Cookware, tableware and refrigeration
  • Guest marketing and concierge sales
  • Booking support and a weather backup location

Closing Vision

A kitchen that could exist nowhere else

SALT Kitchen is in development. We are seeking thoughtful villa, resort and producer partners to help shape it.
